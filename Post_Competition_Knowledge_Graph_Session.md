## ğŸ¨ Teaching Tips

**For Teresa's Learning Style:**
1. **Make it visual** - Use colors, shapes, spatial arrangement
2. **Make it interactive** - She draws, not just watches
3. **Pattern-first** - "See the structure?" not "Memorize this"
4. **Intellectual challenge** - Frame as puzzle-solving, not practice
5. **Quick wins** - Start simple, build complexity

**Engagement Strategies:**
- Ask "What do you notice?" (pattern recognition strength)
- Let her discover connections (not tell her)
- Celebrate "aha!" moments
- Connect to her interests (efficiency, smart shortcuts)

**Avoid:**
- Long lectures (she gets bored)
- Repetitive exercises (goes against her style)
- Abstract without concrete examples
- Making it feel like homework
